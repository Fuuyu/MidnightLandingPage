FAQ

How is the store's equipment?

Our store always checks whether the items as well as tables and chairs are enough, whether any one is broken, fully cleaned to prepare for what is best for customers.

Our food is certified from ISO 22000, which is the first international standard to implement a certified food safety management system. It includes interactive communication, system management and hazard control.
